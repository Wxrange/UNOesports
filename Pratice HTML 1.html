Participation requirements

Qualifications

  1. Read the read-this-first discord channel on the UNO Discord
  2. Any UNO student can join the channels matching your games interest.
  3. Can hold up to 6 teams of 3 with subs
  4. You must follow the code of conduct when particapating in the UNO discord and on the esport team.
  5. You must currently be a UNO student or going to be a UNO student.
  6. You must go to tryouts in order to qualify for joining our esports teams.
